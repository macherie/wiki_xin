**S3202 Touch Controller**

Overview：
          2.7V to 3.6V supply voltage 
          1.62V to 3.6V host interface voltage 
          1.8V core supply for low power operation 
          Up to 10-finger detection and simultaneous tracking 
          Up to 333 kHz sensing frequency 
          Up to 250 Hz report rate; other rates configurable 
          Small finger support (down to 4 mm diameter) 
          Point contact passive stylus input with automatic pen/finger disambiguation 
          I2C (100/400 kHz)
          I2C Slave Address 0x20
          VREG_L19
          VREG_LVS1
          GPIO_INT  17 high to low edge
          GPIO_RST  16
