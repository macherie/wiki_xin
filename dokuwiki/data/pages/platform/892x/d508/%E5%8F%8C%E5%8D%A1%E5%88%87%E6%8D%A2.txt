=====D508切换成双卡DSDA版本修改方法=====

=====AP修改:=====

adb shell setprop persist.radio.multisim.config dsda

=====NV修改:=====

NV 70266 to DSDA

NV 6876 -> dual_Standby_pref to 5

NV 6907 to 1

NV 67256 to 0

NV 70210 hw_config.UIM[1].DISABLE_UIM to FALSE
